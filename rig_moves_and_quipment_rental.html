ERVCAF GRP is a reliable partner for our customers.

We provide mainly specialized and experienced staff services to help and assist our customers in their drilling exploration operations, pipeline, and trucks for heavy equipment on rental basis.

We provide the tools and equipment necessary to help you in your project.

  • These services cover moves from one area to another or in field moves and include:

    • Identification of the required handling and transportation equipment based on Rig’s packing list.
    • Visit the site and move the identification of the route.
    • Risk Assessment and risk identification.
    • Submission for review and approval of a rigging move plan.
    • mobilization of equipment.
    • Rig Move Coordination on the loading site and the unloading site.

  • These services cover trucks and heavy equipment rentals, including:

    • 4 × 4 rental Toyota Land Cruiser Station and pick-ups fully equipped for desert trips.
    • Our cars are equipped with double spare wheels, air compressor, Roll Over bars, fire extinguisher, sand Rail, first aid kit, cargo holding nets.
    • They can be made available with a certified driver for desert and defensive driving or without a chauffeur depending on the rental period.
    • Services of certified freight transport units.
    • Rental of freight transport units certified with appropriate slings.

  • We provide, under these services, mainly specialized and experienced personnel to assist and assist our clients in their exploration and pipeline drilling operations:

    • Logistics Operations Personnel.
    • Welders certified according to AWS AD ASMI standards.
    • Drilling field staff.
    • Drilling Engineers (IWCF & IADC).
    • Engineers completions (IWCF & IADC).
    • Drills and Toopushers (IWCF and IADC).
    • Hands.
    • Restaurants.
    • The Roomboys.
