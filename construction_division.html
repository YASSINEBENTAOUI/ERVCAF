Ervcaf Tecmat JV is one of the recognized construction company in Africa.

As a full service, fully integrated firm, Ervcaf Tecmat designs, engineers, and constructs our clients’ projects from beginning to end with our own expert and caring hands. As a major employer of field services personnel and skilled craft, we wear hardhats, operate cranes, and weld pipe. We do all this and more; touching numerous industries.

Services

Ervcaf Tecmat JV has experience meeting aggressive and complex schedules, and we offer a number of alternative delivery processes for public works and civil projects. In addition to Steel structure and Piping construction, we provides a complete range of field construction services, for clients in both the public and private sectors.

In everything we build for our clients all over Morocco and Africa, safety is our number one priority.

  • Steel structure, Tanks and Industrial Piping:

    • Engineering, supply, manufacture and installation of tanks and storage tanks in black steel or stainless steel and according to the different standards and building codes (API 650, CODRES, etc.).
    • Supply, manufacture and installation of industrial piping in all types of grades (AC, stainless steel, alloy … etc.).
    • Our teams are familiar with the different standards of piping construction such as ASME and CODETI.
    • Manufacture of motorized sliding doors with long range (up to 100m long mega door). Assembly and installation of the production plant.
    • Manufacture and installation of all metal structures.

  • Our hangar doors are designed to withstand fluctuations in moisture levels and temperature; they are resistant to dirt, dust and wear, and stand up to even the most violent winds.

    The TectMat model features two insulation layers to aid with heat and noise insulation. The width and height is not limited thanks to the elevating support post system; it is a great choice for extra wide and extra high doorways for aircrafts.


    WHY USE OUR TECMAT HANGAR DOOR?

    Our hangar doors are lightweight but durable. They do not need extra heavy support structures like many other hoist-up fabric hangar doors on the market, which significantly helps reduce the construction costs.
