About Us

Ervcaf Grp is a company specialized in designing, Manufacturing and operating production, and storage facilities for the oil and gas industry and other energy sources across Africa.

Ervcaf Grp offers different types of study and work solutions for professionals throughout projects phases. These services are grouped together in four business lines (Engineering, Construction, Environment, and Oil and Gas). These solutions meet the diverse needs of public and private clients, from design to completion of turnkey construction and/or maintenance projects based on the synergy of the Group’s subsidiaries when necessary.
